Questions & Answers

How is this different from other spin brushes?

Most "spin scrubbers" are low-torque consumer toys — they spin fast but stall the moment you press them against tile. CleanSpin's TorqueLift™ system maintains rotational force under pressure, which is what actually lifts grime out of porous grout instead of just polishing the surface.

Is it safe on glass and finished surfaces?

Yes. The polish head is rated safe on shower glass, glossy tile, stainless steel, and chrome fixtures. The flat and corner heads are safe on standard ceramic and porcelain. We recommend the deep-bristle head only for grout, stone, and outdoor surfaces.
